## Common Issues

### My wallet won't connect

- Make sure MetaMask is installed and unlocked
- Check that you are on a supported network — see [Add Avalanche Networks](../quickstart/avalanche-network) or [Add XRPL EVM Networks](../quickstart/xrpl-network)
- Try refreshing the page and reconnecting

### I don't have enough USDC

- Follow the [Add USDC guide](../quickstart/add-usdc) to import USDC into MetaMask
- For testnet USDC, use the [Circle Faucet](https://faucet.circle.com) (Fuji and Sepolia)

### My transaction failed

- Check that you have enough native gas token (AVAX or XRP) in your wallet
- Try increasing the gas limit in MetaMask's advanced settings
- On Avalanche Fuji or XRPL testnet, RPC instability can occasionally cause failures — wait a moment and retry

### I submitted a certificate but it is not showing in governance

- Certificate processing can take up to a minute after the transaction confirms
- Check that your wallet holds `REGISTRY_ROLE` — if not, contact NCRB to arrange registration
- Review the transaction on the block explorer to confirm it succeeded

### My certificate was rejected

- Governance members record a rejection reason on-chain — check the proposal detail in the Registry Portal
- Common reasons: quality score below 50, unverifiable certificate document, missing methodology information
- Correct the issue and resubmit as a new certificate

### I cannot see my tokens after purchase

- Make sure you are on the correct network in MetaMask
- Go to **MyNCRB → Dashboard** — balances are fetched from the RWA token contracts
- If the balance is still missing after a few minutes, [contact us](https://www.ncrb.world/contact)
