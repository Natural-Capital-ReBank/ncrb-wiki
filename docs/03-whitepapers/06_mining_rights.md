## 3. Quality Bands & Pricing

Mining rights quality follows internationally recognized reserve classification standards, assessing both economic viability and geological confidence:

| Resource Category | Reserve Status | Confidence Level | Valuation Multiplier | NCRB Tokenization |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Proven Reserves** | Economic extraction | Measured resources, highest confidence | 100% (baseline) | ✅ Eligible |
| **Probable Reserves** | Economic extraction | Indicated/Measured, moderate confidence | 60–80% | ✅ Eligible |
| **Measured Resources** | Pre-economic | Detailed sampling, location known | 40–60% | ⚠️ Review Required |
| **Indicated Resources** | Pre-economic | Adequate sampling, reasonable confidence | 20–40% | ⚠️ Review Required |
| **Inferred Resources** | Pre-economic | Limited sampling, low confidence | 5–20% | ❌ Not Eligible |

### 3.1 Ore Grade Impact (Example: Gold)

- **High grade (>10 g/t):** Premium 200–400%
- **Medium grade (3–10 g/t):** Standard pricing
- **Low grade (<3 g/t):** Discount 30–50%

## 4. Token Issuance Criteria

To qualify for NCRB tokenization, mining rights must meet all of the following criteria:

- **NI 43-101** (Canada), **JORC** (Australia), or **S-K 1300** (US) compliant technical report
- Minimum **Probable Reserves or Measured Resources**
- **Independent Qualified Person (QP) certification**
- **Ore grade** within economic extraction parameters
- **Infrastructure access** and jurisdiction risk assessment

## 5. Quality & Impact Framework

NCRB employs a programmatic quality scoring system (0–100) across six weighted dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | Key Inputs |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Technical Quality | 25% | Reserve category, ore grade, QP certification, technical report quality |
| Additionality | 20% | Economic viability, infrastructure access, permitting status |
| Permanence | 20% | Mine life estimate, reserve depletion rate, royalty term |
| Certification Level | 15% | NI 43-101/JORC/S-K 1300 compliance, jurisdiction regulatory standing |
| Social Impact | 12% | Local employment, community benefit agreements, Indigenous consultation |
| Vintage/Condition | 8% | Technical report recency, updated resource estimates |
