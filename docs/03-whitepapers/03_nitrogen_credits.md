## Abstract

This whitepaper presents Natural Capital ReBank's (NCRB) approach to tokenizing Nitrogen Credits on blockchain. Nitrogen pollution from agricultural runoff is one of the most significant water quality challenges globally, creating a $80 billion market for verified nitrogen reduction credits growing at 18% CAGR. NCRB provides the infrastructure to tokenize, trade, and retire nitrogen credits with full watershed delivery modeling and compliance documentation.

## 3. Quality Bands & Pricing

Nitrogen credit quality is determined by the **delivery ratio** — a measure of how much of the reduced nitrogen load actually reaches the regulated water body, based on its position in the watershed.

| Quality Band | Delivery Ratio | Source Type | Price Range | NCRB Tokenization |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **High Delivery** | 0.8–1.0 (near bay) | Point source WWT | $20–$50/kg N | ✅ Eligible |
| **Medium Delivery** | 0.5–0.8 (mid-watershed) | Agricultural BMP | $10–$30/kg N | ✅ Eligible |
| **Standard Delivery** | 0.3–0.5 (upstream) | Cover crops, conservation | $5–$15/kg N | ✅ Eligible |
| **Low Delivery** | &lt;0.3 (far upstream) | Upland agricultural | $3–$10/kg N | ⚠️ Trading Ratio Applied |

## 4. Token Issuance Criteria

To qualify for NCRB tokenization, nitrogen credits must meet all of the following criteria:

- **State program certification** (PA DEP, MD MDE, VA DEQ)
- **Third-party verification** required
- **Delivery ratio calculation** based on Chesapeake Bay Model Phase 6
- **Trading ratio applied:** 2:1 for non-point to point source trades
- **Annual trading window:** October–November for compliance year

## 5. Quality & Impact Framework

NCRB employs a programmatic quality scoring system (0–100) across six weighted dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | Key Inputs |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Technical Quality | 25% | Delivery ratio, source type, watershed modeling accuracy |
| Additionality | 20% | BMP additionality vs baseline agricultural practices |
| Permanence | 20% | Duration of BMP implementation, maintenance commitments |
| Certification Level | 15% | State certification tier, verification frequency |
| Social Impact | 12% | Farmer income support, rural community water access |
| Vintage/Condition | 8% | Compliance year, monitoring data freshness |
