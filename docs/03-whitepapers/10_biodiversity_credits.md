## Abstract

This whitepaper presents Natural Capital ReBank's (NCRB) approach to tokenizing Biodiversity Credits on blockchain. The biodiversity credit market is one of the fastest-growing environmental markets, valued at $600 billion with 20% CAGR, driven by the Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework's 30x30 targets and mandatory Biodiversity Net Gain (BNG) requirements emerging across multiple jurisdictions. NCRB provides institutional-grade infrastructure to tokenize verified biodiversity credits using the UK Biodiversity Metric, NatureQuant scoring, and Endangered Species Act banking frameworks.

## 4. Token Issuance Criteria

To qualify for NCRB tokenization, biodiversity credits must meet all of the following criteria:

- **Biodiversity Net Gain (BNG) verification** under UK Metric 4.0 or equivalent
- **Third-party assessment** (NatureQuant, Terrasos, or Verra SD VISta)
- **Minimum D3 distinctiveness** or equivalent
- **30-year conservation covenant** or longer
- **Monitoring plan** with annual reporting
- **No net loss principle** compliance

## 5. Quality & Impact Framework

NCRB employs a programmatic quality scoring system (0–100) across six weighted dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | Key Inputs |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Technical Quality | 25% | Distinctiveness score, habitat type, species presence, baseline assessment |
| Additionality | 20% | Net gain above baseline, no net loss demonstration |
| Permanence | 20% | Conservation covenant duration (30-year minimum), legal enforceability |
| Certification Level | 15% | Assessment body tier, BNG verification, Verra/NatureQuant certification |
| Social Impact | 12% | Indigenous land rights, community co-benefits, public access |
| Vintage/Condition | 8% | Assessment recency, monitoring data freshness, habitat trend |

## 7. SDG/ESG Impact Tracking

NCRB tracks the following impact metrics for biodiversity credits:

- **Habitat Restored:** Hectares by habitat type and distinctiveness level
- **Species Protection:** Number of species protected, endangered species coverage
- **Conservation Duration:** Years of binding covenant
- **Biodiversity Net Gain:** Biodiversity units gained per development project

**CSRD/TNFD Mapping:** Biodiversity metrics align with CSRD E4 (Biodiversity & Ecosystems) and all four TNFD pillars: Governance, Strategy, Risk Management, and Metrics & Targets.
