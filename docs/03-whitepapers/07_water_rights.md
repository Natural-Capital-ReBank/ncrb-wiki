## Abstract

This whitepaper presents Natural Capital ReBank's (NCRB) approach to tokenizing Water Rights on blockchain. Water scarcity is one of the defining challenges of the 21st century, and the water rights market — valued at $350 billion with 8% CAGR — is increasingly recognized as a critical investment category. NCRB provides the infrastructure to tokenize verified water rights with full priority-date documentation, transferability verification, and quantified annual allocation, enabling institutional-grade water market participation.

## 4. Token Issuance Criteria

To qualify for NCRB tokenization, water rights must meet all of the following criteria:

- **State Water Resources Board certification**
- **Priority date documentation** (senior rights preferred)
- **Transferability verification** under state water law
- **Quantified annual allocation** (acre-feet/year)
- **No history of forfeiture or non-use penalties**

## 5. Quality & Impact Framework

NCRB employs a programmatic quality scoring system (0–100) across six weighted dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | Key Inputs |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Technical Quality | 25% | Priority date seniority, use type, quantified allocation |
| Additionality | 20% | Water conservation commitments, efficiency improvements |
| Permanence | 20% | Right permanence, historical non-forfeiture, transferability |
| Certification Level | 15% | State board certification tier, registry documentation |
