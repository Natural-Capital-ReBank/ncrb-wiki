# Install MetaMask

MetaMask is a browser extension and mobile app that acts as your Web3 wallet. You need it to connect to the NCRB platform, hold tokens, and sign transactions.

---

## Step 1 — Install the Extension

1. Open your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge)
2. Go to [metamask.io](https://metamask.io) and click **Download**
3. Click **Install MetaMask for [your browser]** — this takes you to the official browser extension store
4. Click **Add to [browser]** and confirm the installation prompt
5. Pin the MetaMask extension to your toolbar for easy access

> **Security:** Only ever download MetaMask from [metamask.io](https://metamask.io) or the official browser extension store. Never install from a third-party link.

---

## Step 2 — Create a New Wallet

1. Click the MetaMask fox icon in your toolbar
2. Click **Create a new wallet**
3. Accept the terms and create a strong password — this protects MetaMask on this device
4. Click **Secure my wallet (recommended)**
5. MetaMask will show you your **Secret Recovery Phrase** — 12 words in a specific order

### Backing up your Secret Recovery Phrase

:::warning
Your Secret Recovery Phrase is the **only** way to recover your wallet if you lose access to this device. Anyone who has these 12 words has full access to your funds. **Write it down on paper and store it somewhere safe offline. Never share it with anyone.**
:::

6. Write down all 12 words **in order** on paper
7. Click **Next** and confirm the phrase by selecting the words in the correct sequence
8. Click **Done** — your wallet is ready

---

## Step 3 — Find Your Wallet Address

Your wallet address is your on-chain identity on NCRB.

1. Open MetaMask
2. Click the account name at the top (e.g. "Account 1")
3. Your address appears — it starts with `0x` and is 42 characters long
4. Click it to copy it to your clipboard
