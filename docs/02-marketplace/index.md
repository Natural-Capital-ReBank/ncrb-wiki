# Marketplace Guides

The NCRB marketplace connects four types of participants. Find your guide below.

---

## Who Are You?

| Role | What You Do | Guide |
|---|---|---|
| **Registry Partner** | Submit verified environmental certificates for tokenization | [Registry Guide](./registry) |
| **Governance Member** | Review and vote to approve certificate submissions | [Governance Guide](./governance) |
| **Buyer / Trader** | Browse, purchase, and retire RWA credits | [Buyer Guide](./buyers) |
| **Admin** | Manage accounts and assign roles across the platform | [Admin Guide](./admin) |

---

## How the Platform Works — End to End

```
Registry submits certificate
        │
        ▼
Oracle API scores quality (0–100)
        │
        ▼
Governance votes (3-of-5 multi-sig, 7-day window)
        │
        ▼
Tokens minted and distributed to recipients
        │
        ▼
Tokens listed on the marketplace
        │
        ▼
Buyers purchase and retire credits on-chain
```
